# Product and Process Dissent Lens

## Check

- **Outcome:** Does this improve what users experience, or only the internal construct the team prefers?
- **Adoption:** Who must change behavior, and why would they?
- **Process tax:** What recurring coordination, approval, or maintenance burden does this create?
- **Opportunity cost:** What higher-value path becomes unavailable if this proceeds?
- **Feedback:** What signal would prove this decision is failing early enough to reverse or adjust?

## Response Shape

- Tie objections to user-visible outcome or team operating cost.
- Challenge internal elegance when it does not change adoption or delivery.
- Define the earliest observable failure signal.
